About Me

I am a student at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, studying Creative Technology. This degree combines Computer Science theory with Electrical Engineering and Design. I am currently focusing on bridging the gap between digital code and the physical world through sensor technology. In my free time I love experimenting with new technologies and working on personal projects that challenge my skills and creativity.

From a young age, I have been fascinated by how things work and enjoy taking things apart and putting them back together in new ways. This curiosity has driven me to pursue a career in technology, where I can continue to learn and innovate.
